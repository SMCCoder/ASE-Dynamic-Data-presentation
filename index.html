The Harms Lab

  • The Harms lab uses coding to explore the relationship between the biophysical properties of proteins and their evolution.
  • The lab's purpose is to learn about things such as, why do some protiens with certain properties and sequences occur? Or how does the physical properties shape their evolutionary trajectories?
  • One of the main things the lab explores is sequence space.

Interactive Data Visualizations Are Important

A regular bar graph, without interaction will show the data in way that is easy to see, but sometimes you need more that to fully understand it.

Bar graph without interaction

Interactiveness can add a lot to a bar graph - for example, you can be able to sort the bars, see popups that show infomation about the bar, update the data, highlight the bar, and see real time data.
